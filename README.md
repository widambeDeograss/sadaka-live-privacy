# Privacy Policy for Sadaka Live

**Last Updated:** June 28, 2026

Welcome to Sadaka Live! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. Sadaka Live is designed to help church congregants view their contributions and giving records for their respective churches. Protecting your privacy and the sensitive nature of financial and personal information is our top priority.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with access to your contribution records, we may collect the following types of information:

- **Personal Information You Provide:** When you create an account or link to your church's records, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, and a password. This helps us identify you and connect you to your contribution records within your church's system.

- **Contribution and Financial Data:** The core function of this app is to display your giving history and contributions. This data is provided to us by your church and is not created or altered by us.

- **Automatically Collected Information:** Like most apps, we automatically collect certain information when you use the app. This may include your device's IP address, device type, operating system, app usage data, and diagnostic information to help us improve the app's performance and security.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **To Provide Our Service:** To allow you to securely view your contribution history and records.
- **To Manage Your Account:** To create and maintain your user account.
- **To Improve Our App:** To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs to enhance your experience.
- **To Communicate with You:** To send you important updates about the app or respond to your support inquiries.

---

## 3. Information Sharing

We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of your data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

- **With Your Church:** Your information is linked to the specific church(es) you are a member of to retrieve your contribution data. We act as a facilitator between you and your church's records.

- **With Service Providers:** We may use trusted third-party companies to help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, data storage, analytics). These partners are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your data to provide services to us.

- **For Legal Reasons:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request (e.g., a court order).

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption (TLS/SSL) for data transmitted between your device and our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

- **Access and Update:** You can access and update your account information (like your email or phone number) within the app's settings.

- **Data Deletion:** You may request the deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us. Please note that your contribution records are managed by your church and may be retained for legal or financial record-keeping purposes.

- **Communications:** You can opt-out of non-essential communications from us.

---

## 6. Minors' Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
